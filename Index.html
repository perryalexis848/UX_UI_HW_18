Gone App

Planning a tour is an ambitious endeavor that involves many moving parts. Smaller, less well-known bands might struggle with the administrative burden a tour represents. Our team wants to help these bands reduce their frustration before, during, and after their travel. The GONE app will connect bandmates, managers, and other personnel by organizing the bookings in one place, allowing them to view or change bookings in the app and have a singular way to communicate.

Our goal was to develop an app for frequent travelers with complex schedules that fosters collaboration on an itinerary.

Egyptian Theater Redesign

The Egyptian Theatre, located in Park City, Utah is a historical non-profit establishment frequented by numerous Sundance Festival Attendees and local patrons year-round. The Initial design lacked coherence and presented challenges in comprehensibility.

Our objective was to strategically redesign it for enhanced organization, visual appeal and navigational ease.
